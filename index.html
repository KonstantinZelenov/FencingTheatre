About me

Hello, my name is Alex Ivanov, and I am thrilled to share my story with you. For the past 20 years, I have dedicated my life to the art of fencing, honing my skills as an athlete, a stunt performer, and a theater and film actor.

I am proud to have been crowned the World Champion in artistic fencing not once, but twice. Additionally, I have secured numerous national titles in Russia, establishing myself as a force to be reckoned with in the realm of artistic fencing.

Throughout my career, I have had the privilege of training a multitude of students who have gone on to become podium finishers in artistic fencing competitions. Seeing their growth and success brings me immense joy and reaffirms my commitment to coaching.

As a fencing coach, I am dedicated to instilling in my students the same passion and drive that has fueled my own journey. I create a training environment that fosters discipline, perseverance, and an unwavering dedication to excellence. Each session is tailored to the individual needs and aspirations of my students, ensuring that they receive personalized guidance and attention.

Beyond coaching, I have also delved into the realm of filmmaking. My expertise as a stunt performer and actor has allowed me to create captivating short video clips showcasing the art of fencing and stage combat. These videos serve as both a source of inspiration for my students and a means to showcase the beauty and excitement of the sport to a wider audience.

My multifaceted background in fencing, stunts, and acting enables me to bring a unique perspective to the training process. I infuse my sessions with elements of theater and performance, encouraging my students to not only become skilled fencers but also captivating storytellers with their swords.

Join me on this extraordinary journey, where we will explore the intricate footwork, masterful techniques, and theatrical brilliance of fencing. Together, we will unlock your true potential and transform you into a formidable fencer. Whether you dream of standing atop a championship podium or simply wish to experience the thrill of this age-old art form, I am here to guide you every step of the way.

Contact me today to embark on a transformative fencing adventure. Let's create something extraordinary together!

About Project

Step into a world where artistry and swordplay intertwine. Our video and theatrical projects in artistic fencing go beyond mere combat, through our carefully choreographed fight sequences, we create a visual symphony of movement and skill, captivating viewers with the beauty and intensity of the sport. We strive to ignite a spark within the hearts and minds of those who witness our performances.
Every sword swing, every parry, and every dramatic pause is carefully crafted to convey a narrative that extends beyond the physical realm. We aim to touch the souls of our audience, to leave them pondering long after the final curtain falls or the last frame fades away.
Join us on this artistic journey, where swords become brushes and the stage becomes our canvas. Together, we will create moments of pure magic, where the clash of steel and the power of storytelling converge to leave a lasting impact.

About School

Welcome to our fencing school. Our fencing lessons will not only teach you the basics of technique such as attack, defense and movement, but will also help you develop flexibility, coordination and strength. Each student will receive attention and support in order to achieve maximum results in their development as a swordsman.
We also offer training in stunt art, where you can learn tricks, falls, and all the necessary skills to create spectacular fight scenes on screen or on stage.
We believe that swordsmanship and stuntmanship are not only physical skills, but also the art of self-expression and creativity.
